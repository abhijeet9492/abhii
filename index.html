📚 Course Modules Overview

✅ Module 1: Introduction to the Cafe Industry

Understand the types of cafes, market size, organized vs. unorganized sector, and trends shaping the industry.

✅ Module 2: Research & Market Analysis

Learn customer behavior, perform SWOT analysis, and define your Unique Selling Proposition (USP).

✅ Module 3: Location Selection and Setup

Master the art of choosing the right location, negotiating rent, evaluating competition, and physical space planning.

✅ Module 4: Financial Planning and Investment

Understand investment required, monthly budgeting, revenue projection, profit margin, break-even, and funding options.

✅ Module 5: Kitchen Setup and Operations

Plan an efficient kitchen layout, essential equipment list, hiring chefs and helpers, managing operations and reducing food waste.

✅ Module 6: Hiring and Team Management

Know how to build the right team, write job descriptions, conduct interviews, onboard staff, and manage shifts.

✅ Module 7: Menu Design and Pricing

Create a high-margin, balanced menu based on your target audience and cafe theme. Includes menu engineering tips.

✅ Module 8: Marketing (Online + Offline)

Launch powerful low-cost promotions, social media marketing, influencer strategies, local ads, and WhatsApp campaigns.

✅ Module 9: Growth and Expansion

Scale your business through new outlets, franchising, or cloud kitchens. Learn how to fund and manage growth.

✅ Module 10: Legal and Compliance

Understand essential licenses like FSSAI, GST, Fire Safety, and how to stay compliant.

✅ Module 11: Crisis Management & Problem Solving

What to do when sales dip? Cost control, new product ideas, influencer marketing, and customer retention tips.

✅ Module 12: Success Roadmap for Café

The long-term strategy: branding, financial planning, sustainability, innovation, and team-building for lasting success.
